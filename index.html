About the School

Therapists for a New Paradigm

We train clinicians to work safely and skillfully with holotropic states of consciousness. Weekly online lectures, breathwork intensives, and mentored practice weave together transpersonal psychology, Grof® Breathwork, and integrative methods.

Our team is rooted in EUROTAS standards and decades of facilitation experience. Join as a full student or select individual seminars that deepen your practice.

  • Transpersonal counseling & Grof®/Holotropic Breathwork
  • 120-minute weekly online lectures with tutors and peer forum
  • In-person intensives across Western and Eastern Europe

Why Train With Us?

Experiential Core

Holotropic and Grof® Breathwork, gestalt, and bodywork are practiced in every intensive for real embodied learning.

EUROTAS Standards

Pathways toward EUROTAS transpersonal psychotherapist certification with supervised practice and ethics.

International Network

Cooperations with Freiburg, Milan, Watertown MA, Estonia, Latvia, and Romania for shared seminars and exchanges.

Flexible Participation

Enroll as a full student, join late with make-up intensives, or purchase individual lectures that fit your focus.
