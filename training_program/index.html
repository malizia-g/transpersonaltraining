Training Philosophy

We recognize that healing and consciousness development require integration of psychotherapeutic expertise with understanding of transpersonal dimensions of human experience. Our training prepares practitioners who are capable of working with clients across the full spectrum of psychological and spiritual needs; from symptom relief to consciousness expansion and authentic self-realization. This requires synchronized development of rigorous psychotherapeutic skills and deep personal understanding of different states of consciousness.

Program Overview

Our postgraduate program in transpersonal psychotherapy training represents a holistic approach to developing professional competence in transpersonal psychotherapy and Holotropic Breathwork. This program is designed not only for mental health professionals, educators, and practitioners but as well as, individuals motivated for self-development who are seeking to deepen their theoretical understanding and psychotherapeutic application of transpersonal psychology; a field that recognizes the spiritual dimension as integral to healing, psychological well-being and self-development.

Transpersonal psychotherapy training integrates contemporary research in psychology with established wisdom traditions, creating a comprehensive framework for understanding human consciousness, transformation, and healing. Our approach to holistic therapy acknowledges that self-development is not relying on symptomatic relief alone, but rather a process of psychological maturation and spiritual awakening that supports individuals steering through their hero journey; the archetypal process of personal transformation and growth.
