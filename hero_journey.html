13 Steps to Wholeness

Our Hero's Journey

A comprehensive 4-year evolutionary path. From the foundations of counseling to the peaks of transpersonal experience.

1

The Foundation

Therapeutic Approaches

The journey begins. Overview of psychotherapeutic approaches, CBT, Humanistic approach, and the fundamentals of Analytical Psychology (Jung): Shadow, projections, and patterns.

2

System & Process

Systemic & Gestalt Work

Resource-based Systemic Therapy, Process Oriented Therapy, and Gestalt Work. Double Holotropic Breathwork sessions in Todtmoos.

3

Evolution of the Psyche

Effective Psychotherapy

Creative Evolution 2.0. Relationships as evolutionary practice. The 12 ancient practices of caring for the soul. The Hero's Journey.

4

Archetypes & Energy

Biotransenergetics I

Deep dive into Archetypes and the foundations of Biotransenergetics (BTE) with Dr. Pier Luigi Lattuada. Film study: "The Secret of Carlos Castaneda".

5

The Inner Child

Hypnotherapy & BTE II

Continuation of Biotransenergetics. Introduction to Hypnotherapy and working with the Inner Child. Healing the Ego and understanding Stan Grof's life work.

6

Maps of the Psyche

Constellations & Psychosynthesis

Strategic sessions, Family Constellations (Orders of Love), Psychosynthesis, and Stan Grof's expanded maps of consciousness.

7

Holotropic States

Transpersonal Psychotherapy

Collective Trauma, Kundalini, Spiritual Journey via Heart-based Meditation. Regression Therapy as a healing tool. Perpetrator-Victim structure.

8

Forbidden Wisdom

Psychedelics & Perinatal

Healing potential of Holotropic states. Perinatal Matrices. Psychedelics as a source of spiritual experience: dangers, recreation, and therapeutic settings.

9

Alchemy of Trauma

Psychopathology

Love in Psychotherapy. Forging transpersonal gold from life's deepest wounds. Gestalt Chair Work. Spiritual Emergency vs Spiritual Resistance.

10

Breath & Sound

Setting & Music

Individual Constellations in the group. How to work with a group. Setting and concept of Holotropic Breathwork. The role of Music in Breathwork.

11

Death & Rebirth

Integration Therapy

Strategy of Holotropic Sessions. Death of the Ego in Stan Grof's work. Integration Therapy and Family Constellations.

12

Ancient & Modern

Shamanism & Integral Theory

The Psychology of Shamanism. Psychopathology from Grof's point of view. Deep dive into Ken Wilber's Integral Theory.

13

Practice & Completion

Supervision & Ethics

Ethics in Holotropic states. Art Therapy. Dealing with Shame, Fear, and Guilt. Spiritual crisis vs psychic disorder. Final Exams and Supervision.
