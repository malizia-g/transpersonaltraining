Curriculum

A structured progression from self-development through professional facilitation to advanced psychotherapy and Holotropic Breathwork practice.

Home Training Curriculum

Our Hero Journey

Level 1

Self-Development & Consciousness Exploration

4 modules
1

Foundations of Transpersonal Psychology & Consciousness

History and core concepts of transpersonal psychology. Major schools of thought, debates within the field, and positioning transpersonal psychology within contemporary psychology and mental health systems. Introduction to consciousness studies and models of self and identity.

2

Science of Consciousness & Self-Regulation

Basics of science relevant to therapeutic work: nervous system regulation, stress physiology, emotion regulation, default mode network, and neurobiological correlates of meditation and breathwork. Developing personal self-regulation skills.

3

Non-Ordinary States & Holotropic Breathwork (Foundations)

Holotropic paradigm, set and setting, session structure, contraindications, breather/sitter roles, safety protocols, and experiential introduction to non-ordinary states within an ethical container.

4

Spiritual Development, Crisis & Integration

Models of spiritual development across cultures. Spiritual emergence vs. psychological crisis. Phenomenology of mystical experiences and dark night processes. Foundations of integration: grounding, meaning-making, embodiment, and psychological containment.

Level 2

Counselling Skills & Professional Facilitation

5 modules
5

Clinical Foundations: Theories of Psychotherapy & Transpersonal Integration

Overview of major psychotherapy models (psychodynamic, attachment-based, humanistic-existential, somatic, trauma-informed). How clinical models conceptualize suffering and change. Integrating clinical theory with transpersonal perspectives.

6

Counselling Skills, Therapeutic Relationship & Presence

Core counselling skills: empathic listening, attunement, reflective responding, emotion-focused work, establishing therapeutic alliance, boundaries, and basic assessment. Working with relational dynamics and ethical presence in one-to-one and group contexts.

7

Facilitation of Non-Ordinary States & Group Process

Professional facilitation of breathwork and consciousness practices: session design, music sequencing, non-directive bodywork principles, containment of emotional material, group dynamics, and post-session integration practices.

8

Professional Ethics, Boundaries & Continuing Development

Deepening ethical practice for professional transpersonal work. Maintaining clear professional boundaries while facilitating deep consciousness work. Learning when and how to seek supervision and consultation. Establishing ethical, sustainable professional practices with emphasis on the concept of consent and bodywork.

9

Somatic Awareness, Trauma-Informed Practice & Integration

Nervous system regulation, trauma-aware facilitation, grounding and stabilization skills, working with dissociation and overwhelm, and integration practices that support psychological safety and continuity of change.

Level 3

Psychotherapy Skills & Holotropic Breathwork Practitioner

5 modules
10

Advanced Psychotherapeutic Skills & Clinical Process

Advanced therapeutic skills: deep listening, moment-to-moment process tracking, rupture and repair, transference/countertransference, pacing depth, working with resistance, and maintaining therapeutic alliance in complex clinical work.

11

Transpersonal Interventions & Tools in Psychotherapy

Clinical use of transpersonal tools: imaginal work, symbolic dialogue, archetypal processes, ritual elements, therapeutic use of spiritual frameworks, dreamwork, and meaning-oriented interventions. Indications, contraindications, and ethical application in psychotherapy.

12

Trauma-Informed Transpersonal Psychotherapy

Advanced work with developmental trauma, attachment injury, dissociation, and shame-based organization. Integrating somatic regulation, parts-based models, and consciousness-oriented interventions within phase-oriented trauma treatment.

13

Clinical Assessment, Differential Diagnosis & Treatment Planning

Advanced assessment integrating diagnosis manuals-informed diagnosis with transpersonal perspectives. Differential diagnosis between spiritual emergence, trauma responses, dissociation, and psychiatric pathology. Phase-oriented treatment planning, risk management, and collaboration with mental health professionals.

14

Consciousness-Based Psychotherapy & Holotropic Breathwork (Advanced Integration)

Clinical integration of Holotropic Breathwork and non-ordinary state work within longer-term psychotherapy. Indications, contraindications, clinical timing, integration planning, working with grief, existential crisis, identity transformation, and sustaining therapeutic outcomes beyond peak experiences.

Eligibility

General Eligibility (All Levels)

Applicants should demonstrate:

  • • Master's Degree in relevant field (Required for those aiming to get certified)
  • • Emotional stability and psychological readiness to engage in intensive experiential and reflective learning
  • • Capacity for self-reflection, personal responsibility, and relational maturity
  • • Openness to working with altered states of consciousness within a structured, ethical, and supervised context
  • • Willingness to engage in personal therapy and supervision as part of professional formation

Note: This training is experiential and may involve deep emotional and psychological processes. Applicants with acute, unmanaged psychiatric conditions that could be destabilized by non-ordinary states of consciousness may be advised to defer participation until clinically appropriate.

Admissions

Level 1 — Open Admission

Who Can Apply: Anyone interested in transpersonal psychology, consciousness exploration, and personal development. No formal prerequisites required.

Requirements

  1. Completed application form with personal statement
  2. Brief CV or professional background
  3. Psychological Biography
  4. Phone/video interview with program coordinator
  5. Demonstrated interest in consciousness and personal growth
Level 2 — Counseling Track

Requirements

  1. Level 1 completion documentation
  2. Personal statement describing professional goals
  3. Professional references (minimum 2)
  4. Interview with program coordinator
  5. Commitment to personal ongoing development and supervision
Level 3 — Psychotherapy Track

Requirements

  1. Level 1 and 2 completions
  2. Minimum 1-year clinical professional experience
  3. Documented transpersonal or consciousness-based work experience
  4. Clinical supervisor recommendation
  5. Detailed professional statement and clinical goals
  6. Comprehensive interview with faculty panel
  7. Commitment to ongoing personal therapy and supervision
