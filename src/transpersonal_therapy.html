What is Transpersonal Therapy?

Transpersonal therapy is an integrative approach that honors the full spectrum of human experience, including spiritual, mystical, and transcendent dimensions of consciousness.

It goes beyond traditional therapeutic models by recognizing that healing and growth can emerge from experiences that transcend the individual ego and connect us to something larger than ourselves.

This approach integrates insights from depth psychology, meditation practices, breathwork, shamanic traditions, and contemporary neuroscience to support profound transformation and wholeness.

Core Principles

Holistic Perspective

Viewing the person as an interconnected whole—body, mind, emotions, and spirit.

Transcendent Potential

Honoring experiences that go beyond ordinary consciousness and personal identity.

Inner Healing Intelligence

Trusting the inherent wisdom of the psyche to guide its own healing process.

Spiritual Integration

Integrating spiritual experiences and insights into everyday life and personal growth.
