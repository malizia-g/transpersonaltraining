## Biography

President of the Association of Transpersonal Development in Moldova and certified transpersonal psychotherapist. She leads the Eastern European program with extensive experience in transpersonal psychology and breathwork. Board member of EUROTAS and coordinator of the Ethics Committee. Her approach combines traditional Eastern wisdom with modern transpersonal methodologies, exploring shamanic states of consciousness, spiritual crisis, and quantum processes on individual and collective consciousness.
