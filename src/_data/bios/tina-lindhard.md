## Biography

Senior core teacher with extensive experience in meditation, craniosacral therapy, and transpersonal psychology. Based in Spain, she is an accomplished author, researcher, and public speaker on consciousness, meditation, health, and wellness. With a background spanning psychology, philosophy, and consciousness studies, she brings deep theoretical and experiential knowledge to breathwork psychotherapy. Mentor for MA and PhD students at IUPS.
