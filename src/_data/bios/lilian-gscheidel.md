## Biography

Core faculty member specializing in breathwork psychotherapy, gestalt therapy, and dance therapy. Works in a private practice for gestalt therapy in Berlin and offers Grof Breathwork® weekends. Living in community for many years, she brings a deeply relational and embodied approach to therapeutic work, integrating movement, breath, and consciousness exploration.
