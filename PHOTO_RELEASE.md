## Release Agreement

I, the undersigned ("**Releasor**"), hereby grant to **Transpersonal Training** (the "**Organization**"), its affiliates, successors, and assigns, the irrevocable and unrestricted right and permission to:

### 1. Use of Images

- Photograph, record, and/or film me during training sessions, workshops, intensives, lectures, and related events.
- Use, reproduce, publish, and distribute my image, likeness, voice, and/or statements in any and all media, whether now known or hereafter developed, including but not limited to:
  - **Website** (transpersonaltraining.com and any successor domains)
  - **Social media** platforms (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, X/Twitter, and others)
  - **Marketing materials** (brochures, flyers, posters, banners, business cards)
  - **Digital communications** (newsletters, email campaigns, online advertisements)
  - **Print publications** (magazines, newspapers, journals)
  - **Presentations** (conferences, webinars, online courses)
  - **Video content** (promotional videos, documentaries, training recordings)

### 2. Editing Rights

The Organization may crop, alter, retouch, composite, or otherwise edit the images as it sees fit, provided that such alterations do not materially misrepresent the Releasor or place the Releasor in a false light.

### 3. No Compensation

I acknowledge that I will not receive any financial compensation, royalty, or other payment for the use of my image under this release, now or in the future.

### 4. No Prior Approval Required

I waive any right to inspect or approve the finished product, the advertising copy, or any other matter in connection with the use of my image.

### 5. Duration

This release is valid **in perpetuity** from the date of signing, unless revoked in writing as described in Section 8 below.

### 6. Ownership

All photographs, videos, and other media produced remain the exclusive property of the Organization. I do not claim any ownership rights over these materials.

### 7. Representations and Warranties

- I am at least **18 years of age** and legally competent to sign this release.
- I am signing this release **voluntarily** and of my own free will.
- I have read and understand the terms of this release in their entirety.

### 8. Revocation

I understand that I may revoke this consent at any time by submitting a **written request** (email or postal mail) to the Organization. Upon receipt of such revocation:

- The Organization will make reasonable efforts to **cease future use** of my image.
- The Organization is **not obligated to remove** images already published, distributed, or incorporated into existing materials prior to the revocation date.
- Revocation does not apply retroactively to materials already in circulation.

### 9. Limitation of Liability

I release and hold harmless the Organization, its employees, agents, contractors, and representatives from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action arising out of or related to the use of my image as authorized herein.

### 10. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Organization is registered.
