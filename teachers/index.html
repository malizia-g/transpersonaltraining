Core Teacher

Prof. Jure Biechonski

Core Teacher

Expert in transpersonal psychology and academic research

Professor Jure Biechonski brings academic rigor and deep theoretical knowledge to our program. Based in Estonia, he is a renowned researcher and practitioner in transpersonal psychology, bridging the gap between academic research and practical application in therapeutic settings.

His work focuses on integrating transpersonal psychology principles with contemporary psychotherapeutic approaches, emphasizing the importance of consciousness studies in modern therapeutic practice. With extensive experience in both academic research and clinical applications, he offers unique insights into the theoretical foundations and practical implementations of transpersonal methods.

  • Professor in Transpersonal Psychology
  • Academic Researcher in Consciousness Studies
  • Certified Transpersonal Psychotherapist
  • Expert in Holotropic Breathwork Theory and Practice
  • Academic Teaching in Transpersonal Psychology, Estonia
  • Research in Consciousness Studies and Altered States
  • Integration of Academic Theory with Clinical Practice
  • Facilitating Transpersonal Workshops and Seminars
